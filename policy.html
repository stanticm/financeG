Subscription Policy

The Subscription price can be found in the "Pricing Table" listed below, the amount and interval you will be charged depends on your Country and Product selection. The cards accepted are Visa and MasterCard. Once you subscribe, the following e-mails will be sent to the e-mail address that you register with:


  • An invoice for the trial period.
  • A reminder e-mail detailing the start date of your regular monthly billing cycle & a copy of our terms and conditions.
  • A welcome e-mail with your login credentials.

You can cancel subscription any time and your profile will stay active until the next billing cycle. If you decide to cancel your subscription you can do it by logging into your account and clicking "Cancel Membership", or you can contact our support team via e-mail at: support@codegenc.com or by phone at: +44 330 808 5502.
ACTINVITE LIMITED reserves the right to change the price of your Subscriptions, by posting the new price on its Service. Price changes for Subscriptions will take effect at the start of the next subscription period following the date of the price change. As permitted by local law, you accept the new price by continuing to use the Service after the price change takes effect. If you do not agree with the price changes, you have the right to reject the change by unsubscribing from the Service prior to the price change going into effect. Please therefore make sure you read any such notification of price changes carefully.
If you do not cancel your membership by the time your trial period expires, your card will be charged the regular monthly subscription fee. Subscription prices by Country can be found in “Pricing by country” table which can be found in our Terms and Conditions.

You may cancel the services by giving ACTINVITE LIMITED written notice via the email: support@codegenc.com or via contacting the phone number: +44 330 808 5502 or by logging in to your account and canceling your subscription.

Should you wish to do so, you may pause your subscription for an unlimited duration for any reason. For further information please contact support.
If you cancel your subscription, your membership will stay active until the next billing cycle. Once the billing cycle is finished, you will no longer have access to your profile and we may delete all of your user content. We accept no liability for such deleted information or content.
